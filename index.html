ABOUT ME

DOM

I'm a story teller who performs analysis. I make words with numbers and art with words. With graduation immenent I am on the lookout for work opportunities. I am expecting to graduate with at 3.3 in Statistics and 3.2 in Computer Science this May, 2020. Although application development is a skill I have, I am more interested in the analytical side of the workforce. Data Analysis roles that will grant me exposure to how data science is conducted are my desired positions. I do believe however that experience can be attained from multiple environments. If I've sparked your curiosity please reach out. Sometimes I like to branch out and spend my time more creatively. Recently, I have taken up the hobbies of painting and writing. Although the writing will not appear on this website, I am posting a gallery of the paintings that I have and will paint until this virus situation calms down.
